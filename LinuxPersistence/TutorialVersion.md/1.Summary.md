## :one: Summary

:large_blue_circle::white_circle::white_circle::white_circle::white_circle::white_circle::white_circle::white_circle:

This tutorial will help you flash the Ubuntu Linux operating system onto a USB flash. Unlike with just a live version, this guide will allow you to have a live version of linux but accompanied with persistent storage. After going through this tutorial, you will have a portable OS drive that you can get used to working with before installing the full version.
